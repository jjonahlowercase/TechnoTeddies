Story

The planet Corvus was a civilized world where Teddies and Lizards lived in harmony for centuries. This was until the techno revolution which led to the Teddies adopting the new name Techno Teddies. The Lizards were afraid of this new technology and refused to participate in the techno revolution.

Over time the Lizards grew jealous of the Techno Teddies and their new technology, which led to the great war of 2012. The Lizards were no match for the Techno Teddies, who emerged victorious. With their home wartorn and destroyed, the Teddies were forced to leave and take refuge in the nearest planet called Earth. Blending into society with humans proved to be a difficult challenge, but they managed. Now 2022, the Techno Teddies live a peaceful life on Earth knowing that the Lizard species died out with the war, or so they thought…
